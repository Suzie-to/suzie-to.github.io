BOOTCAMP

Test your front-end programming knowledge and determine if you have what it takes to join the prestigious bootcamp. Get ready to showcase your skills and save the bootcamp from disintegration!

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to guess the secret word within the chosen category by guessing one letter at a time. You have to unveil the entire word before the bootcamp disintegrates due to your wrong guesses. You only have six chances, so use them wisely!

Are you an HTML Hero, a Javascript Jedi or a CSS Sensei, pick your category and showcase your knowledge!
